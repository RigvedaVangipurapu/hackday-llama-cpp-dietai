HEALTHY RECIPES AND MEAL IDEAS

A balanced diet includes a variety of nutrient-dense foods prepared in healthy ways. Here are meal ideas organized by macronutrient focus.

HIGH-PROTEIN MEALS
- Grilled chicken breast with quinoa and steamed broccoli (approx. 40g protein, 35g carbs, 10g fat, 400 calories)
- Baked salmon with sweet potato and asparagus (approx. 35g protein, 30g carbs, 15g fat, 380 calories)
- Greek yogurt parfait with berries and nuts (approx. 20g protein, 25g carbs, 12g fat, 280 calories)
- Lentil curry with brown rice (approx. 18g protein, 45g carbs, 8g fat, 320 calories)
- Tofu stir-fry with mixed vegetables (approx. 20g protein, 25g carbs, 10g fat, 280 calories)

BALANCED MEALS
- Mediterranean bowl: Grilled chicken, quinoa, chickpeas, olives, feta, vegetables (approx. 30g protein, 40g carbs, 20g fat, 450 calories)
- Buddha bowl: Roasted vegetables, chickpeas, avocado, tahini dressing, whole grains (approx. 15g protein, 50g carbs, 18g fat, 420 calories)
- Turkey and vegetable wrap with whole grain tortilla (approx. 25g protein, 35g carbs, 12g fat, 350 calories)
- Baked cod with roasted vegetables and brown rice (approx. 30g protein, 40g carbs, 10g fat, 380 calories)

LOW-CARB OPTIONS
- Zucchini noodles with turkey meatballs and marinara (approx. 35g protein, 15g carbs, 18g fat, 350 calories)
- Grilled steak with cauliflower rice and green beans (approx. 40g protein, 10g carbs, 20g fat, 400 calories)
- Egg scramble with vegetables and avocado (approx. 20g protein, 8g carbs, 25g fat, 320 calories)
- Salad with grilled chicken, nuts, and olive oil dressing (approx. 30g protein, 12g carbs, 22g fat, 380 calories)

HIGH-FIBER OPTIONS
- Oatmeal with berries, chia seeds, and nuts (approx. 12g protein, 45g carbs, 15g fat, 350 calories)
- Black bean and vegetable soup with whole grain bread (approx. 18g protein, 50g carbs, 8g fat, 340 calories)
- Whole grain pasta with vegetables and lean protein (approx. 25g protein, 55g carbs, 12g fat, 420 calories)

PREPARATION METHODS
- Steaming: Preserves nutrients, no added fat
- Grilling: Adds flavor, minimal added fat
- Baking: Even cooking, can use minimal oil
- Stir-frying: Quick cooking, use healthy oils sparingly
- Roasting: Enhances flavors, use minimal oil

MEAL PREP TIPS
- Prepare proteins in bulk (chicken, fish, legumes)
- Pre-cut vegetables for easy access
- Cook grains in larger batches
- Portion snacks in advance
- Keep healthy dressings and sauces ready
